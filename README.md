## Show, don't tell

Quoting a booth off a spec sheet is a hard sell. AIM AV lets you drop in a floor plan, drag equipment from a library of displays, kiosks, staging, lighting, and furniture, and hand your client a photorealistic preview of their actual booth — in minutes, not a design-team ticket.

Load the space → place the gear → render it real. That's the whole workflow.

## Built for how AV teams actually work

- **No fixed SKUs to hunt through.** Every item in the library is freely resizable, so one "flat panel display" covers a 43" through an 85" — no scrolling a catalog of near-identical models.
- **One click to photoreal.** Your layout gets sent to AI rendering that adds real shadows, materials, and lighting while keeping every item exactly where you put it.
- **The bill of materials builds itself.** Whatever's on the canvas gets tallied automatically — no separate spreadsheet to keep in sync.
- **Nothing to install, nothing to lose.** Runs entirely in your browser, saves as you go, works offline once loaded. Close the tab mid-layout and it's exactly where you left it when you come back.
- **Your data stays yours.** No account, no server, no vendor holding your projects hostage. The only thing that ever leaves your browser is the render request you trigger yourself.
